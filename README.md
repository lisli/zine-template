Numbered sections in markup correspond to zine pages; they are reordered via flex order to print with zine pages in the correct order when folded in traditional 8 page zine format. 


To Print
========

Open the index.html page in your browser, then print.

- Landscape, not portrait
- Made for US Letter-size paper (feel free to adjust all `in` values in the source for A4 or whatever you need; you might need to bump positions of things around accordingly)
- Print with 0.25in margins on all sides
- No header or footer (why do browsers do that??)
- Use a paper cutter or scissors to cut off the margins, then fold into a zine:
